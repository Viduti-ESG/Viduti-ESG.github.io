Frequently asked questions

Can I try Pro before paying? +
Yes. Contact us on LinkedIn or WhatsApp and we'll give you a 14-day Pro trial with no payment required. We want you to see the value before committing.
What payment methods are accepted? +
Currently we accept UPI, NEFT/RTGS, and bank transfer (invoice-based). Razorpay card payments are coming soon. Enterprise clients get 30-day payment terms.
Is the data SEBI-compliant? +
Our data is sourced from public BRSR filings, BSE/NSE corporate disclosures, and MoEFCC/CPCB records. Green Curve is not a SEBI-registered ESG Rating Provider — we are an analytics and workflow platform. AI-generated assessments are indicative, not formal ratings.
How often is the data updated? +
Company ESG scores are updated quarterly based on BRSR filing cycles. BRSR filing status is checked weekly. GHG estimates refresh weekly. The ESG event feed refreshes daily from SEBI, NGT, and MoEFCC.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time with no penalty. Annual plans are non-refundable after the first 30 days, but you keep access until the end of your billing period.
Do you offer discounts for NGOs or academic institutions? +
Yes — we offer 50% discounts for registered NGOs, universities, and SEBI-accredited research institutions. Contact us with proof of status.
