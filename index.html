What is Viduti ESG?

Viduti ESG is an automated intelligence platform tracking regulatory and market developments at the intersection of business and climate transition.

Every night, our agent scrapes official portals — CPCB, SEBI, MoEFCC, IFRS, EFRAG, and the GHG Protocol — extracts compliance documents, and generates plain-English analysis using Claude AI, so Indian corporates and sustainability professionals wake up to actionable intelligence, not raw PDFs.
