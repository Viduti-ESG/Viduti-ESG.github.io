Compliant by architecture, not by afterthought.

Green Curve Bookings was designed against India's DPDP Act 2023 and aligned with GDPR principles — so you can offer scheduling to your clients without inheriting legal risk.

Full details in the Booking Terms & Privacy Notice.

  • Explicit consent, recorded. Invitees actively consent to a versioned privacy notice and terms before anything is stored — timestamped for audit.
  • Data minimisation. Name, email, optional notes. Nothing else is asked; nothing else is kept.
  • Right to erasure, self-serve. Every confirmation email carries a private link to cancel or permanently delete personal data — no support ticket needed.
  • Automatic retention limits. Invitee personal data is anonymised 180 days after the meeting ends.
  • Tracker-free surface. The public booking page loads zero analytics or advertising scripts. Data stays in India on Green Curve infrastructure.
