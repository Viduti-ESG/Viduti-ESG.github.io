# Financial Risk Summary: Metro Brands Limited Metro Brands faces critical compliance exposure under SEBI's BRSR Core expansion with a perfect 10.0/10 compliance risk score, creating potential enforcement action and delisting risk for listed entities—a material governance threat to its ₹2,449.6 crore revenue base.
