AI Risk Summary

**Financial Risk Summary – Mufin Green Finance Limited** Mufin Green Finance faces critical compliance exposure under SEBI BRSR Core Expansion mandates, with a maximum compliance risk score of 10.0/10 creating potential enforcement action and delisting risk for a top-tier NBFI; this represents the company's largest financial vulnerability. The company's estimated compliance cost of ₹0–1 crore significantly understates likely regulatory remediation expenses, as unclear EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) applicability (6.5/10 exposure) and absent Scope 1/2 emissions quantification indicate incomplete ESG disclosure infrastructure rather than genuine absence of environmental footprint. As an NBFI financing SME operations, Mufin Green must urgently clarify regulatory obligations under BRSR P6, validate EPR applicability to financed businesses, and establish baseline emissions accounting—failure risks regulatory penalties, investor erosion, and potential credit rating downgrades given India's tightening ESG compliance framework.
