**SPACENET ENTERPRISES INDIA LIMITED – FINANCIAL RISK SUMMARY** Spacenet faces moderate ESG financial exposure primarily through Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) obligations, with a 6.5/10 EPR exposure score indicating undefined commodity-specific waste management liabilities that could materialize as unquantified compliance costs once regulatory clarity emerges. Under SEBI BRSR Core Expansion rules, non-disclosure or misstatement of environmental metrics creates enforcement and potential delisting risk; with estimated compliance costs of ₹0–1 crore currently, formal EPR applicability confirmation and emissions baseline establishment are urgent to avoid regulatory penalties. Primary action: clarify EPR applicability by commodity type and establish transparent Scope 3 emissions inventory to de-risk BRSR disclosure compliance.
