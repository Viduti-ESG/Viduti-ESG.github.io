AI Risk Summary

**Financial Risk Summary: Som Distilleries & Breweries Limited** Som Distilleries faces material compliance risk with estimated remediation costs of ₹4–17 crore, driven primarily by critical gaps in BRSR Core Expansion disclosures (compliance risk scored 10.0/10) and undefined Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) obligations in the beverage packaging segment—exposures that could trigger SEBI enforcement action or delisting for a ₹2,831 crore revenue company. The absence of reported Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions data represents either a measurement and reporting failure or a significant transparency gap; given water intensity risk of 5.0/10, the company faces dual exposure under BRSR P6 environmental disclosures and potential India Green Credit Programme participation requirements. Immediate priority should be clarifying EPR applicability and bottling material streams, quantifying Scope 1 emissions from manufacturing and logistics operations, and establishing baseline water accounting—collectively essential to avoid regulatory penalties and meet institutional investor ESG mandates increasingly embedded in Indian capital markets.
