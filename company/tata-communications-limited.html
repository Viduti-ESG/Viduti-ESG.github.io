AI Risk Summary

**TATA COMMUNICATIONS LIMITED – FINANCIAL RISK SUMMARY** Tata Communications faces critical compliance risk (10.0/10) under SEBI BRSR Core Expansion mandates, with potential enforcement action and delisting exposure for non-compliance on mandatory environmental disclosures—a material governance liability for a listed telecom operator. The company's EPR (Extended Producer Responsibility) exposure (6.5/10) is elevated but unquantified; with applicability status unknown, remediation costs could range ₹10–50 crore depending on e-waste and telecommunications equipment scope. GHG and water intensity risks (both 5.0/10) remain moderate but material given sector infrastructure footprint; absent Scope 1/2 emissions data, the company cannot benchmark against India's carbon credit market (₹600–900/tonne) or justify climate strategy to investors. Immediate priority: file complete BRSR P6 disclosures and clarify EPR applicability to avoid regulatory penalties and valuation erosion.
