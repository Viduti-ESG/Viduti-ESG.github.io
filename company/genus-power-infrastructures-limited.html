**Financial Risk Summary: Genus Power Infrastructures Limited** Genus faces critical compliance exposure with a 10.0/10 compliance risk score and estimated remediation costs of ₹4–15 crore against ₹2,442 crore revenue (0.16–0.61% of topline), driven primarily by unclear EPR applicability (6.5/10 risk) and potential SEBI BRSR non-disclosure penalties including delisting risk for non-compliance with Core Expansion mandates. The company's failure to report Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions data—despite operating in the electronics/communications sector where indirect emissions tracking is material—suggests either inadequate measurement systems or disclosure gaps that violate BRSR P6 environmental disclosure obligations. Without confirmed EPR registration and emissions quantification, Genus cannot accurately assess carbon liability under India's emerging carbon credit trading scheme (CCTS ₹600–900/tonne) or future carbon tax exposure, creating material financial uncertainty in transition planning and regulatory standing with stock exchange authorities.
