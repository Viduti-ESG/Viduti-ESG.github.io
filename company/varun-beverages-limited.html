# Varun Beverages Limited – Financial Risk Summary Varun Beverages faces its largest financial exposure through non-compliance with SEBI BRSR Core Expansion mandates, with an estimated cost of ₹23–87 crore to remediate governance and disclosure gaps; the compliance risk score of 10.0/10 indicates material delisting and enforcement action risk if ESG reporting standards are not met. Water intensity represents a secondary structural risk given the beverage sector's operational dependence on freshwater sourcing—absent quantified Scope 1/2 emissions data suggests incomplete GHG accounting that could trigger regulatory scrutiny and carbon pricing exposure at ₹600–900/tonne CO2e once disclosed. The company must prioritize BRSR P6 environmental disclosure completeness and establish baseline GHG/water accounting systems to mitigate compliance penalties and protect market valuation across the ₹14,556.8 crore revenue base.
