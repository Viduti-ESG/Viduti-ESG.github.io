AI Risk Summary

**Financial Risk Summary: Tamilnad Mercantile Bank Limited** Tamilnad Mercantile Bank faces its most acute financial exposure through compliance risk (10.0/10), with estimated remediation costs of ₹10–37 crore, primarily driven by gaps in SEBI BRSR Core Expansion reporting requirements and environmental disclosure obligations under BRSR P6. The bank's undefined Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) applicability (6.5/10 exposure) creates contingent liability uncertainty; if EPR becomes applicable to banking operations or supply chains, additional compliance infrastructure and financial provisioning could be required. Under SEBI's enforcement framework, non-compliance with mandatory BRSR disclosures poses delisting risk for BSE/NSE-listed entities and potential regulatory penalties, creating material downside risk to market valuation beyond the ₹10–37 crore direct cost estimate. Immediate priority: clarify EPR applicability scope and formalize BRSR P6 disclosure protocols to mitigate enforcement action likelihood and quantify true compliance exposure.
