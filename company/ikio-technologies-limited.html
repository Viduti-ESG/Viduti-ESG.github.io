AI Risk Summary

**IKIO TECHNOLOGIES LIMITED – FINANCIAL RISK SUMMARY** IKIO's highest financial exposure stems from non-compliance with SEBI BRSR Core Expansion requirements, with a critical compliance risk score of 9.3/10 that could trigger enforcement actions or delisting—a material threat to its ₹207.7 crore revenue base. Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) obligations carry elevated exposure (6.5/10) with undefined applicability; if manufacturing electrical/transport equipment qualifies under e-waste or plastic rules, regulatory non-compliance could incur penalties and remediation costs exceeding the estimated ₹0–1 crore buffer. The company's missing Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions data creates dual risk: inability to demonstrate climate risk disclosure compliance under BRSR P6 and exposure to potential carbon pricing liability (₹600–900/tonne) once emissions are quantified, particularly if machinery manufacturing involves significant Scope 2 grid consumption. Immediate action required on BRSR attestation, EPR classification mapping, and emissions baseline establishment to mitigate regulatory escalation.
