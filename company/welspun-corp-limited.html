AI Risk Summary

**Welspun Corp Limited – Financial Risk Summary** Welspun faces critical compliance exposure worth ₹14–53 crore annually, driven by maximal compliance risk (10.0/10 score) under SEBI BRSR Core Expansion mandates and India's Carbon Credit Trading Scheme (CCTS), where missed emissions targets trigger penalties of ₹1,200–1,800/tonne—double the current market rate of ₹600–900/tonne. The company's steel exports to the EU, already declining 24.4% YoY, face additional pressure from the EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM), requiring 15–22% price cuts to remain competitive; a mid-sized exporter shipping 50,000 tonnes incurs €3–4 million in annual CBAM costs due to India's average carbon intensity (2.5 tCO2/tonne steel) exceeding EU baselines. With missing Scope 1/2 emissions data and ambiguous EPR applicability (6.5/10 exposure), Welspun risks SEBI enforcement action
