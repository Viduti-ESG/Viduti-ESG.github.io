AI Risk Summary

**Financial Risk Summary – Control Print Limited** Control Print faces material compliance risk under SEBI's BRSR Core Expansion framework, with a critical 10.0/10 compliance score indicating significant gaps in environmental and governance disclosures that could trigger enforcement action or reputational penalties. Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) exposure of 6.5/10 is the primary financial concern—as a printing and media reproduction company, CPL likely falls under plastic and paper waste EPR obligations, with estimated remediation costs of ₹1–2 crore annually if liabilities are not proactively managed. The company's reported zero Scope 1 and 2 emissions data raises verification credibility concerns; even modest energy use in printing operations should generate measurable emissions, suggesting either incomplete data collection or disclosure deficiency that regulators may challenge. Immediate priority should be: (1) audit and quantify actual emissions baseline, (2) map EPR applicability and establish compliance roadmap, and (3) implement BRSR P6 disclosure protocols to mitigate enforcement and stakeholder confidence risks.
