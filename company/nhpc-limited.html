**NHPC Limited - Financial Risk Summary** NHPC Limited's financial risk profile is characterized by a relatively low ESG risk score of 3.5 out of 10, indicating a manageable level of environmental and social concerns. However, the company's EPR exposure score of 4.8 out of 10 suggests a moderate level of risk associated with environmental pollution. The estimated remediation cost band is ₹15–57 crore, which could be a significant burden if not managed effectively. The top risk factors include waste management, board diversity, and water intensity. With a compliance risk score of 0.0 out of 10, the company demonstrates strong regulatory compliance. Immediate priority: Enhance waste management practices to mitigate EPR exposure and reduce the estimated remediation cost.
