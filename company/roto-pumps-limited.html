**Financial Risk Summary – Roto Pumps Limited** Roto Pumps faces critical compliance exposure under SEBI BRSR Core Expansion, with a maximum compliance risk score of 10.0/10 creating potential enforcement and delisting risk if mandatory disclosures are not met—particularly given the company's ₹240.4 crore revenue scale. Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) liability represents the largest unquantified financial threat (6.5/10 risk), as applicability remains unknown; if pumps or components fall under notified EPR categories, the company could face collection and recycling cost obligations of ₹20–50 lakhs annually depending on product volume. Estimated compliance remediation costs of ₹0–1 crore are understated given the absence of reported Scope 1/2 emissions data, suggesting either material underreporting or data gaps that could trigger regulatory penalties. Immediate priority: clarify EPR applicability and establish baseline GHG/water accounting to avoid escalating compliance costs and reputational damage under India's tightening environmental disclosure regime.
