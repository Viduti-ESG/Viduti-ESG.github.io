Climate Transition Plan

Turn your own BRSR data into a credible, framework-aligned Climate Transition Action Plan — structured on the TPT / IFRS S2 framework. Green Curve reviews every plan before it's released, so nothing leaves the door unchecked.

Sign in to build a transition plan

Your CTAP drafts are private to your Green Curve account. Sign in or create an account →

This tool prepares a draft Climate Transition Action Plan from the data you provide. Every forward-looking statement is your company's own commitment, subject to your review and approval. The plan is not assured and is not a statement of regulatory compliance. Green Curve reviews each draft against a credibility checklist before release — it does not rate, score, or assure the plan.
